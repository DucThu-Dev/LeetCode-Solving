## Tips

- **Stuck after 20 min?** Look at the approach, then code it yourself from scratch.
- **Pattern > memorization** — understand *why* a sliding window works, not just the code.
- **Keep a pattern cheat sheet** as you go — it becomes your pre-interview review.
- **Easy problems still matter** — they build speed and confidence for the real thing.
